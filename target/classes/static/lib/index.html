The "Aguerooooo" moment is arguably one of the most iconic moments in Manchester City's history. It occurred on May 13, 2012, during the final matchday of the 2011/2012 Premier League season. Manchester City was facing Queens Park Rangers at the Etihad Stadium, needing a win to secure their first league title in 44 years. As the game entered stoppage time, Manchester City trailed 2-1, with rivals Manchester United leading in their match against Sunderland. In the 93rd minute, Edin Dzeko scored a crucial equalizer for City, giving them hope of a comeback. Then, in the 94th minute, Sergio Aguero received a pass from Mario Balotelli, dribbled past a QPR defender, and scored a dramatic, last-minute goal to clinch the 3-2 victory and the Premier League title for Manchester City. The sheer drama, tension, and euphoria of Aguero's goal, coupled with Martin Tyler's iconic "Aguerooooo" commentary, made it a moment that will forever be etched in Manchester City's history and the hearts of their fans. It marked the end of a long title drought and ushered in a new era of success for the club under manager Roberto Mancini.
